Privacy Policy for Lizard App

Effective Date: August 11, 2025

Information We Collect

Lizard App is designed to be a simple, fun clicking game. We do not directly collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users.

Third-Party Services

AdMob by Google

Our app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use information to provide personalized ads. This may include:

  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • Ad interaction data
  • General location information (country/region level)

For more information about Google's privacy practices, please review Google's Privacy Policy

Data Storage and Transmission

Lizard App stores and may transmit the following information:

Information Stored Locally:

  • Your click count and game progress
  • Game settings and preferences

Information We May Collect and Store Online:

  • Your high scores and click counts (for leaderboard features)
  • General gameplay statistics
  • Device type and operating system (for compatibility)

How We Use This Information:

  • To display your position on game leaderboards
  • To track global game statistics
  • To improve game performance and features
  • We do not collect personal information such as names, emails, or contact details

All leaderboard data is anonymous and only includes your score and general device information.

Children's Privacy

Lizard App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the app store listing and within the app.
